# Multilingual Care Navigation
Summary: A useful health assistant should adapt language and next-step instructions to the caregiver context, not just the symptom list.

People often need support in their own language, especially when they are stressed or helping a child or older family member. The app should ask which language the caregiver prefers and whether instructions should be simplified.

When specialist care is far away, the assistant should focus on the next safe action: home monitoring, same-day clinical advice, or emergency escalation.

This makes the product easier to demonstrate in the hackathon because the value is obvious even before any advanced fine-tuning.
