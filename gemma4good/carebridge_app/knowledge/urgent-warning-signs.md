# Urgent Warning Signs
Summary: Red-flag symptoms should override ordinary home monitoring and push the user toward urgent or emergency care.

Watch for severe warning signs such as trouble breathing, chest pain, blue lips, confusion, fainting, seizures, or someone becoming hard to wake up.

If these appear, a text-only assistant should stop pretending the issue can be safely managed at home. The safer behavior is to recommend emergency services or immediate in-person evaluation.

In lower-connectivity settings, the app should still give short, clear escalation language and avoid complicated instructions.
