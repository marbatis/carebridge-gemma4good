# Fever And Respiratory Support
Summary: Common fever and cough symptoms can often be watched closely at home, but worsening breathing or persistent high fever needs faster escalation.

For fever, cough, sore throat, or body aches without red flags, useful home guidance includes rest, hydration, and tracking whether symptoms are improving or worsening over time.

If breathing becomes harder, the fever remains high for several days, or the person cannot keep fluids down, the app should move from general support to same-day clinical advice.

Users should understand what to monitor next instead of receiving a vague answer. Useful follow-up questions include duration of symptoms, fluid intake, and whether there are higher-risk conditions.
