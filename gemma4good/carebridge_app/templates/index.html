Gemma 4 Good Prototype

CareBridge

A maternal-and-family resilience assistant for the United States: grounded guidance for postpartum danger signs, child fever and dehydration, and heat or smoke-driven breathing risk when clinics are far, internet is weak, or a caregiver has to decide fast.

U.S. Relevance

The product is built around what is straining American families now: maternal care deserts, pediatric access gaps, wildfire smoke, heat stress, aging caregivers, and households that need bilingual, plain-language escalation guidance.

Future Generations

The long-term value is not another generic chatbot. It is a household resilience layer: a tool that helps families notice red flags sooner, document what matters, and hand off clean context to clinicians before delays become harm.
